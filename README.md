# Marketing Channel ROI Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the performance of different marketing channels used by an e-commerce business. The goal is to evaluate which channels generate the most revenue, which channels acquire the most customers, and which marketing strategies are the most cost-efficient.
This analysis focuses on understanding the return on investment (ROI) and customer acquisition efficiency of each channel to help businesses make better marketing decisions.

---

## Business Problem

E-commerce companies invest marketing budgets across multiple channels such as Instagram, Facebook Ads, Google Ads, Email marketing, and Organic traffic.However, not all channels generate profitable results. Some channels may bring many orders but require high marketing costs, while others generate fewer orders but deliver higher return on investment (ROI).
The objective of this analysis is to evaluate the effectiveness and profitability of different marketing channels by analyzing revenue, orders, customer acquisition, and marketing costs.This analysis helps businesses decide where to increase marketing investment and where to reduce spending.

## Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze marketing performance and answer these questions:

- Which marketing channels generate the highest revenue?
- Which channels generate the most orders?
- Which channels bring the highest units sold?
- Which marketing channels have the highest cost?
- Which channels provide the highest return on investment (ROI)?
- What is the customer acquisition cost (CAC) for each channel?

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The following marketing metrics were calculated:

- Revenue by Channel  
- Orders by Channel  
- Units Sold by Channel  
- Marketing Cost by Channel  
- Return on Investment (ROI)  
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)


---

## Key Insights

1 - Email marketing generates the highest ROI (73.6) due to extremely low marketing costs, making it the most profitable channel.

2  - Instagram produces the highest revenue and largest number of orders, indicating strong performance in customer acquisition.

3 - Organic traffic generates significant revenue without marketing cost, highlighting the importance of SEO and brand visibility.

4 - Google Ads and Facebook Ads generate strong revenue but require higher marketing investment compared to Email marketing.

5 - Email marketing has the lowest customer acquisition cost, making it the most cost-efficient marketing channel. 

6 - Instagram marketing has the  second highest customer acquisition cost , making it the least efficient marketing channel.

7 - Google Ads has a higher CAC, meaning it is more expensive to acquire customers through paid advertising.
---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in **Email marketing campaigns** due to their high ROI and low customer acquisition cost.

- Optimize **Instagram advertising costs** while maintaining its strong customer acquisition performance.

- Invest in **SEO and organic marketing strategies** to grow cost-free traffic.

- Monitor and optimize **Google Ads and Facebook Ads campaigns** to improve marketing efficiency.
